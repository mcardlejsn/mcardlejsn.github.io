The Problem

The Gap No One Talks About

Most organizations have software for documentation, medication management, and HR. But when a resident is in crisis at 2am, your DSPs are on their own.

No in-the-moment guidance. No fast way to capture what happened. Just experience, instinct, and hope that the details get remembered correctly hours later when shift notes are finally written.

The DSP Suite was built to fill that gap — by a Direct Support Professional who lived it every shift.

"I built the tools I wished I had during every difficult shift. Fast, private, and designed for the real demands of the job." — A Direct Support Professional & Creator of the DSP Suite

The Real Cost

The Real Cost No One's Pricing In

Direct support turnover isn't just a scheduling headache — it's expensive, and it's constant. Every DSP who walks away means recruiting, retraining, and overtime to cover the gap, on top of the toll it takes on the residents who counted on them.

The parts of this job that wear staff down fastest aren't always the hard moments — they're the paperwork, the "did I do this right," the scramble to remember details hours later. The DSP Suite is built to take weight off exactly there.

Less friction on shift means new hires ramp faster — and the staff you already have spend more time on the work that matters, not the forms around it.
