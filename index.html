Integration

Not a Replacement. A Complement.

Your organization already has software for documentation, medication management, and HR. DSP Buddy doesn't replace any of it.

It's the tool staff use during the incident — before they sit down to document formally. Think of it as the bridge between the moment and the paperwork.

No complex onboarding. No IT setup required. No threat to existing systems. Staff are using it on day one.

The Workflow

🔴 Incident occurs → DSP opens DSP Buddy

✅ Action steps → Immediate guidance on screen

📋 Log the incident → Severity, tags, voice note

📄 End of shift → Export PDF, email to supervisor

📁 Formal documentation → Staff opens existing system

Privacy

Your Data Stays Where It Belongs

Unlike apps that rely on cloud-based voice recognition, DSP Buddy processes all voice notes locally on the device using offline AI.

No audio is ever transmitted to Google, Apple, or any third party. No client information ever leaves the device. Ever.

Built offline from day one — not patched for compliance after the fact.
