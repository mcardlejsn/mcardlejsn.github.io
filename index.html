It's the tool staff use during the incident — before they sit down to document formally. Think of it as the bridge between the moment and the paperwork.

Privacy

Your Data Stays Where It Belongs

Unlike apps that rely on cloud-based voice recognition, DSP Buddy processes all voice notes locally on your device using offline speech recognition — no audio ever leaves your phone.

No audio is ever transmitted to Google, Apple, or any third party. No client information ever leaves the device. Ever.

Built offline from day one — not patched for compliance after the fact.
