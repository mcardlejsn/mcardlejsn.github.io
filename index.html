The Problem

The Gap No One Talks About

Most organizations have software for documentation, medication management, and HR. But when a resident is in crisis at 2am, your DSPs are on their own.

No in-the-moment guidance. No fast way to capture what happened. Just experience, instinct, and hope that the details get remembered correctly hours later when shift notes are finally written.

The DSP Suite was built to fill that gap — by a Direct Support Professional who lived it every shift.

Privacy

Your Data Stays Where It Belongs

Unlike apps that rely on cloud-based voice recognition, the DSP Suite processes all voice notes locally on the device using offline speech recognition — no audio ever leaves the phone.

No audio is ever transmitted to Google, Apple, or any third party. No client information ever leaves the device. Ever.

Built offline from day one — not patched for compliance after the fact.
