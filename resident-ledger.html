Resident Ledger

A per-resident mileage and spending tracker: log trips and expenses with category tags, see a running balance, and export it all as a PDF.

Get Started → See all apps ↓

Per-resident mileage and spending, with PDF export

Resident funds and mileage are easy to lose track of and a headache to reconcile. Resident Ledger logs trips with destination and miles, tracks debit and credit transactions with category tags, and shows a derived running balance per resident — all exportable as a clean PDF report.

  • Mileage log — with destination and miles per trip
  • Debit / credit ledger — with category tags
  • Running balance — derived per resident
  • Encrypted database — resident financial records are encrypted at rest
  • PDF export — for supervisor review
  • Fully offline — anonymous identifiers only
