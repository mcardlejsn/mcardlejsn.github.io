DSP Buddy

The flagship app: in-the-moment guidance and incident logging for every shift. Seven color-coded situation categories, immediate action steps, and a full record captured before the details fade.

Incident logging built for the moment it happens

When a resident is in crisis, there's no time to search a documentation system or try to remember it all hours later. DSP Buddy puts the right category one tap away, walks through immediate action steps, and captures what happened in under a minute — voice note included, no signal required.

Everything stays on the device under anonymous resident codes, and exports as a clean PDF to your own email when the shift ends.

  • 7 color-coded categories — aggression, meltdowns, medication refusal, and more, each with structured guidance
  • On-device voice transcription — speak the note hands-free, nothing sent to a server
  • Severity ratings — for a complete record
  • Encrypted database with biometric lock — incident records are encrypted at rest and gated behind fingerprint/face unlock
  • PDF export — straight to your email app
  • Full archive system — across up to 10 residents per home
