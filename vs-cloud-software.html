How It Compares

A complement, not a replacement.

You already have software for documentation, medication management, and HR. The DSP Suite doesn't replace any of it. It's the layer that captures what happens during the shift, before anyone sits at a computer.

Get Started → See all apps ↓

Cloud platforms ask you to migrate your whole operation onto them, adopt a new system of record, train everyone, and pay a subscription that never ends. That's a heavy lift for a small agency — and it still doesn't help the DSP standing in a hallway at 2am with no signal.

The DSP Suite is deliberately narrow. It captures the moment — the incident, the med pass, the goal note — fast, offline, on the device. Then you export and carry those details into whatever formal system you already use.

No migration. No monthly bill. No network connection. It sits in front of your existing tools, not on top of them.

Where each model fits

  • A cloud platform makes sense when you want one system of record for billing, scheduling, and compliance and you have the budget and IT to run it.
  • The DSP Suite fits when your staff need to capture accurate detail on the floor, right now — offline, affordable, nothing to configure. It's built to run alongside whatever system you already use, not replace it.
