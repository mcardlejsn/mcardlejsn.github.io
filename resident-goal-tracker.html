Document goal progress on every shift

No fiddly attempt counters. Goal Tracker keeps it to what matters: for each goal, log whether the resident was independent or prompted, per shift. Quick to enter, easy to review, and exportable as a PDF report for supervisors.

  • Per-resident goals — across up to 10 residents per home
  • Independent / prompted logging — clean, no attempt-tracking clutter
  • PDF reports — for supervisor review
  • Fully offline — anonymous identifiers only
  • Encrypted database — goal and progress data is encrypted at rest
