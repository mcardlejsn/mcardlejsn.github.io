Why a one-time fee, not a monthly bill

Most care software charges per user, per month, forever. For a small agency with turnover, that's a bill that climbs every time you onboard a DSP and never stops. The DSP Suite is the other model: one price per location, paid once. Add as many staff as you want at that site — the price doesn't move.

  • Per location, not per seat. Unlimited DSPs at each home for one flat fee.
  • One-time, not recurring. No subscription, no auto-renew, no contract to escape.
  • Free future updates. New features ship to the apps you already own.
  • 30-day money-back guarantee. If your staff don't find it useful in 30 days, full refund.

Founder pricing for the first 10 agencies is $499 per location for the full suite — one time — versus a $999 standard rate once founder spots fill. Full breakdown, including the Core Pair and individual apps, is on the pricing page.
